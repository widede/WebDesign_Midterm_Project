Scroll Of Taiwu

"Scroll Of Taiwu" is an independent game developed by ConchShip Games with myths and martial arts as its theme. The player will play the mysterious "Tao's biography".This is a very cultural game.

Game features!

"Scroll Of Taiwu" is a pure martial arts style SRPG game. In the world of "Taiwu Painted Scrolls", in addition to playing the mysterious "Taiwu's biography", you will also take a different position on the world - good Or evil, or moderate, devote themselves to the complicated rivers and lakes. You can not only visit the martial arts martial arts around the world, but also learn a wide variety of martial arts skills; you can also join Jinlin with humanity, or form a bloody sea; you can not only build your own village, run various industries; you can also love your own life. Childbearing, fate three life; until you finally face the enemy of Taiwu, defeat the sword, determine the fate of the world!
